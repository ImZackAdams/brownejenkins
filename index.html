About Browne & Jenkins

Browne & Jenkins is a literary publication that delves into the darkest corners of human imagination. We specialize in horror, dark fiction, and experimental works that challenge conventional storytelling and evoke profound, unsettling emotions. Our mission is to bring to light the shadows that lurk in the corners of your mind.
